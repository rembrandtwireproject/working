Automated Overlay Videos

About

Automated Overlay Videos (AOVs) are visual representations that layer harder-to-identify watermarks with previously identified watermarks in order to further bolster or rule out a match. The repository below serves as a reference point for both the successful implementation of this technology and also for the specific findings from examined collections of partner institutions. Our sincere thanks to Drs. C. Richard Johnson and William A. Sethares for their permission to use their watermarkPointMarker and visualizeOverlays tools made possible through the Getty Foundation Research Grant ORG-20194357.
