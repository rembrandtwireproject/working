Automated Overlay Videos

About

An Automated Overlay Video is a visual representation of the overlay matches of harder-to-identify watermarks. These .gif videos fade in and out watermarks of inquiry with previously identified watermarks in order to better represent the presence or absence of a match. The repository below serves as a reference point for both the successful implementation of the technology and also for the specific findings from examined collections of partner institutions. Our great thanks to C. Richard Johnson and William A. Sethares for their permission to use their watermarkPointMarker and visualizeOverlays tools made possible through the Getty Foundation Research Grant ORG-20194357.
